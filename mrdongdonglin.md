# Dongdong Lin

## Bio

Dongdong Lin is currently a lecturer at [Huaqiao University](https://faculty.hqu.edu.cn/ddlin/zh_CN/index.htm), woking with [Hui Tian](https://faculty.hqu.edu.cn/htian/). I received my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees from Xiangtan University, supervised by [Chengqing Li](http://www.chengqingli.com/), and completed my Ph.D. at Shenzhen University, supervised by [Bin Li](https://scholar.google.com.hk/citations?user=g0iR9IkAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN). From 2020 to 2022, he was a visiting student at the University of Siena, Italy, working with [Mauro Barni](https://scholar.google.com.hk/citations?user=ntRScY8AAAAJ&hl=zh-CN) and [Benedetta Tondi](https://scholar.google.com.hk/citations?user=xpNEfq4AAAAJ&hl=zh-CN).

My research focuses on AI security, deep model watermarking, deepfake detection, adversarial robustness, and multimedia forensics. Recent work spans watermarking for diffusion models, speech synthesis systems, and ownership verification of generative models.

I am currently interested in trustworthy AIGC, robust model ownership protection, and security problems around modern multimodal generative systems.

I serve as a CCF Professional Member, a CSIG Member, and an IEEE Member. I serve as a reviewer for IEEE T-IFS/DSC/ASLP, SP, CVPR, ACM MM, and ICASSP.
