About

Profile

Dongdong Lin is a lecturer at Huaqiao University. He received his B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees from Xiangtan University, and completed his Ph.D. at Shenzhen University. From 2020 to 2022, he was a visiting student at the University of Siena, Italy.

His research interests include AI security, cyberspace security, multimedia forensics, deep model watermarking, deepfake detection, adversarial attacks, and cryptanalysis. He has published in venues such as IEEE TDSC, ACM MM, ICASSP, Pattern Recognition, and IJCB.

Research

Topics I Work On

Model Watermarking

Ownership verification and copyright protection for GANs, diffusion models, and speech generation systems.

Deepfake Forensics

Detection methods that leverage spatial, temporal, and artifact-level cues to improve robustness.

Adversarial Robustness

Attack and defense strategies for modern perception systems, especially under transferability and fine-tuning threats.

Cryptanalysis

Security analysis of image ciphers and learning-based approaches to cipher distinguishers and attacks.
