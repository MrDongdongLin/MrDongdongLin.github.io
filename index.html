About Me

Dongdong Lin received his M.Sc. degree and B.Sc. degree with the College of Information Engineering, Xiangtan University, China in 2015 and 2018, respectively. He was a visiting student with the Department of Information Engineering and Mathematics of the University of Siena, Italy, from 2020 to 2022. He is currently pursuing his Ph.D. degree with the College of Electronic and Information Engineering, Shenzhen University, China. His research interests include Deep Model Watermarking, Deepfake Detection, Adversarial Attacks and Cryptanalysis.
